### 1. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Immich app ("we", "our", or "us") and covers our collection, use, and disclosure of your information. This Policy does not cover any third-party websites, services, or applications that can be accessed through our app, or third-party services you may access through Immich.

### 2. Information We Collect

**Locally Stored Data**: Immich stores all your photos, albums, settings, and other metadata locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, nor do we transmit or store it on any of our servers.

**Purchase Information**: When you make a purchase at [buy.immich.app](https://buy.immich.app/) we collect the following information:

- Country of origin
- Postal code (if the user is from Canada or the United States)
- Email address

**Tile Server**: When you make a request to [tiles.immich.cloud](https://tiles.immich.cloud/) we collect the following information about the request:

- Requested tile
- IP address
- Timing metrics

> Note: this is an optional feature which can be disabled [here](https://my.immich.app/admin/system-settings?isOpen=location).

**Version Service**: When you make a request to [version.immich.cloud](https://version.immich.cloud/) we collect the following information about the request:

- User-Agent
- IP address
- Timing metrics

> Note: this is an optional feature which can be disabled [here](https://my.immich.app/admin/system-settings?isOpen=version-check).

### 3. Use of Your Information

**Improving Immich**: Hosted services like the **Tile Server** and **Version Service** collect request metadata in order to better understand our user base and ensures that we optimize the services for those locations and users.

**Purchasing Immich**: The country of origin and postal code (for users from Canada or the United States) are collected solely for determining the applicable tax rates on your purchase. The email address is only used to send the product key purchase confirmation email and verify purchase in the case of a lost product key.

### 4. Sharing of Your Information

**Tax Authorities**: The purchase information may be shared with tax authorities as required by law.
**Payment Providers**: The purchase information may be shared with payment providers where required.

### 5. Security

**Data Security**: We're committed to safeguarding your information. While no system is 100% secure, we take various measures to protect information about you from unauthorized access, use, alteration, or destruction.

### 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any changes, we will notify you by revising the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. It's encouraged that users frequently check this page for any changes to stay informed about how we are helping to protect the personal information we collect.

### 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at [immich@futo.org](mailto:immich@futo.org)
