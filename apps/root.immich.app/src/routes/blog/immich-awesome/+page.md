Hi everyone!

Inspired by the popular `awesome` repositories on GitHub, we decided to make our own list of _awesome_ Immich-related things. The site is live at https://awesome.immich.app/ and currently contains a lists of awesome Immich apps, integrations, tools, distributions, and guides.

---

<Button href="https://awesome.immich.app/" color="secondary" trailingIcon={mdiOpenInNew}>View Awesome Immich</Button>

---

This website replaced the previous `Community projects` and `Community guides` sections of our [documentation](https://docs.immich.app/) website and the pre-existing content was already migrated to the new site. One benefit of moving the content to Immich-Awesome is that it can be deployed _immediately_, while the documentation website is only ever updated when a release happens.

If you know of any project or guide not on the list, feel free to [submit a pull request](https://github.com/immich-app/static-pages/edit/main/apps/awesome.immich.app/src/data/items.json) to get it added.
